Marc Anthony (Ebo)

Painter in Accra/Cape Coast

The painting depicts a man gathering together cash expelled from a money-vomiting serpent. In the Akan dialect, this ritual is known as 'sika duro' and it literally translates as 'money medicine.' It is simply when a covetous person offers blood sacrifice to a deity in return for their kindness.

Decades ago, when printers were inaccessible, movie promoters relied on local artists. In the same way as most movie theatres, the Manso Video cinema in Mamobi-Accra expected an audience to their showings with this fly-poster for Death Wish 3.
