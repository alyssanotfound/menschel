Kate

Kate is a seamstress in Pokuase who makes custom oufits for people who live nearby. We worked with Kate and Edem to produce quilt-like textiles from the secondhand Western clothes that are sold in the country. These photographs were taken in the popular Makola Market in Accra, where the piles of secondhand clothes are humorously called "bend down boutiques."
